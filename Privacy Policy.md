# Privacy Policy 🛡️

Last updated: March 28, 2026

## Overview 📘

GhoulStreams respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website and how that information is handled.

## What GhoulStreams Does 🎬

GhoulStreams does not host media content on its own servers. The site provides links to media hosted by third-party services.

## Information We May Collect 📊

The website may collect limited technical information needed to operate and protect the service, such as:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device and operating system information
- Referrer/URL data
- Basic request and error logs

This information is used for reliability, security, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies 🍪

GhoulStreams may use essential cookies or local storage to support core site functionality, including player preferences and session behavior.

If analytics or other optional tools are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

## Third-Party Services 🌐

Because GhoulStreams links to or loads resources from third-party providers, those services may collect data under their own privacy policies.

GhoulStreams is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services.

## Data Sharing 🤝

GhoulStreams does not sell personal data.

Data may be shared only when required by law, to respond to legal requests, or to protect the security and integrity of the service.

## Data Retention 🗂️

Technical logs are retained only for as long as reasonably needed for operations, security, and troubleshooting.

## Children 👶

This website is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy 🔄

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes become effective when posted on this page.
